Bastardised and butchered generic Karplus-Strong synthesis. Try a Sawtooth, or even a Bell wave.

This is an example of a generator function using another generator function. In this case, karplus_strong wraps around a generator function and applies a poor emulation of a real-world object over it.

attack in seconds decay in seconds sharpness 0-1 is decent sample_rate in hertz (eg, 44_100.0)

use synthrs::wave;

let karplus_sawtooth_generator =
    |frequency: f64| {
        wave::karplus_strong(wave::sawtooth_wave(frequency), 0.01, 1.0, 0.9, 44_100.0)
    };
